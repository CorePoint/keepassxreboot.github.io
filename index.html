KeePassXC

KeePass Cross-Platform Community Edition

The best thing Computers can do is store information.
You shuldn't lose your time trying to remember and type your passwords.
KeePassXC can store your password safely and Autotype them in your everyday websites and applications.

Encrypted

The complete database is always encrypted either with AES (alias Rijndael) encryption algorithm using a 256 bit key. KeePassXC uses a database format that is compatible with KeePass Password Safe. Your wallet always works offline with no internet connection.

Cross-Platform

KeePassXC is a Community fork of KeePassX, the cross-platform port of KeePass for Windows. Every feature is cross-platform and tested to give users the same feel on each operative system, including the loved Autotype feature.
