--- layout: pages title: "The Project" permalink: project --- KeePassXC is a community fork of KeePassX which aims to incorporate stalled pull requests, features, and bug fixes that have never made it into the main KeePassX repository.

Additional Features
  • keepasshttp support for use with PassIFox for Mozilla Firefox and chromeIPass for Google Chrome.

Requirements

The following tools must exist within your PATH:

  • make
  • cmake (>= 2.8.12)
  • g++ (>= 4.7) or clang++ (>= 3.0)

The following libraries are required:

  • Qt 5 (>= 5.2): qtbase and qttools5
  • libgcrypt (>= 1.6)
  • zlib
  • libmicrohttpd
  • libxi, libxtst, qtx11extras (optional for auto-type on X11/Linux)
